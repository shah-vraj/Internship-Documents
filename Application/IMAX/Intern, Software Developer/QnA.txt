What sparked your interest in IMAX?*

IMAX has always stood out to me as a company that pushes the boundaries of cinematic experiences through cutting-edge technology. The first time I watched a movie in IMAX, I was captivated by the sheer scale, clarity, and immersion it provided. As someone with a strong passion for both software development and entertainment technology, I am drawn to how IMAX seamlessly integrates advanced engineering, optics, and software to create unforgettable experiences. The opportunity to contribute to such innovation while learning from industry-leading engineers is what excites me the most.
