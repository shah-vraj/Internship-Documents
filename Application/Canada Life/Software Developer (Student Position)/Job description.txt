Software Developer (Student Position)
Description: 
Temporary Full Time 

-

 

 

This a student position that is 4 months in duration (May - August 2025). You must be a student registered in a Canadian College or University.

This position is for students returning to school after their work term or requiring a placement as requirements for graduation. While not required, preference will be given to students enrolled in a registered Co-op program. 

 

About the Role:
We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Summer Co-op Student to join our Salesforce team. You will gain hands-on experience working with the Salesforce Sales and Service Consoles, supporting real-world projects that enhance agent and advisor experiences. This role is ideal for a student eager to learn about CRM systems, cloud platforms, and agile development.


Key Responsibilities:
• Assist in configuring and customizing Salesforce Sales and Service Consoles to meet business needs.
• Collaborate with business stakeholders, developers, and admins to gather requirements and test new features.
• Support data analysis, reporting, and dashboard creation within Salesforce.
• Participate in troubleshooting and resolving system issues.
• Document solutions, workflows, and best practices to enhance team efficiency.
• Work in an agile environment, contributing to sprint planning and stand-ups.

What You Bring:
• Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business Technology, or a related field.
• Basic understanding of CRM systems, cloud platforms, or relational databases (Salesforce experience is a plus).
• Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
• Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
• Interest in learning Salesforce best practices and industry trends.

Nice to Have:
• Familiarity with Salesforce Lightning Experience, Flows, Apex, or SOQL.
• Exposure to agile methodologies and tools like Jira or Confluence.

Why Join Us?
• Hands-on experience with a leading CRM platform in an insurance industry setting.
• Opportunity to work on impactful projects that support customer service and sales teams.
• Mentorship and learning opportunities from experienced Salesforce professionals.
• A collaborative, supportive team environment that values innovation and growth.
