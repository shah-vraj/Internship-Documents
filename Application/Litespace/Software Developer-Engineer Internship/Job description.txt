Position Overview‍

We are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic Software Developer/Engineer Intern to join our dynamic team. This internship offers current students the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in software development, contributing to real-world projects and collaborating with experienced professionals.



Key Responsibilities

Software Development: Assist in the design, development, testing, and debugging of software applications.
Code Collaboration: Participate in code reviews and contribute to team meetings, providing constructive feedback and suggestions.
Research and Innovation: Stay updated with emerging technologies and propose innovative solutions to enhance existing systems.
Documentation: Document software development processes, code changes, and other technical information.
Cross-Functional Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams to understand project requirements and deliver solutions that meet business needs.


Recommended Qualifications

Education: Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
Programming Skills: Basic knowledge of programming languages such as Java, Python, C++, or JavaScript.
Problem-Solving Abilities: Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with a keen attention to detail.
Communication Skills: Effective written and verbal communication skills.
Team Player: Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
Eagerness to Learn: A proactive attitude with a willingness to learn and adapt to new technologies.


Preferred Skills & Tools

Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
Understanding of software development methodologies such as Agile.
Experience with web development frameworks or tools.
Knowledge of database management systems and SQL.


Location‍

Flexible – Options available for remote work and/or on-site collaboration, depending on candidate location and business needs.
