At Mistplay, we’re building the ultimate loyalty platform for mobile gamers, and our backend services play a crucial role in delivering seamless, high-performance experiences. As a Backend Developer Intern, you’ll gain hands-on experience working with backend technologies while helping to power key features of our platform.

You'll collaborate with experienced engineers to build, optimize, and scale backend services that support millions of users. If you're eager to develop your problem-solving skills, collaborate with a supportive team, and gain hands-on experience in building impactful solutions, this role is for you!

What You’ll Do At Mistplay

Assist in designing, developing, and deploying backend services that support Mistplay’s rewards and engagement platform.

Write and maintain scalable, secure, and high-performance code in TypeScript using NestJS and ExpressJS.

Work with cross-functional teams to integrate APIs and backend services.

Participate in troubleshooting and debugging to maintain system reliability.

Contribute to technical discussions and collaborate with the engineering team to explore AI-driven solutions that enhance personalization and user experience.

What You’ll Bring To Mistplay

Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.

Familiarity with JavaScript/TypeScript and Node.js .

Interest in learning frameworks like NestJS and ExpressJS.

Strong problem-solving skills and a keen eye for clean, maintainable code.

Passion for working in a collaborative, fast-paced environment.

Bonus: Experience with generative AI, prior backend development projects, or familiarity with Git and RESTful APIs.
