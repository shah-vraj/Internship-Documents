At Mistplay, our Android app is the gateway to a world of rewards for mobile gamers. As an Android Developer Intern, you'll work with our mobile team to build and enhance features that keep users engaged while gaining valuable hands-on experience with Kotlin and Android development best practices.

If you’re excited about creating seamless, high-performance mobile experiences and want to work on an app used by millions, this internship is for you!

What You’ll Do At Misplay

Work with the mobile engineering team to design and develop new Android features that enhance our gaming loyalty experience.

Write clean, efficient, and maintainable Kotlin code.

Participate in code reviews, debugging, and testing to ensure the app runs smoothly.

Stay up-to-date with Android development best practices and contribute ideas for app improvements.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI-driven personalization features into the app.

What You’ll Bring To Mistplay

Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.

Basic understanding of Kotlin and Android Studio.

Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Interest in mobile UX/UI and user engagement strategies.

Bonus: Experience with generative AI, previous Android development projects/internships, and familiarity with Git/RESTful APIs.
